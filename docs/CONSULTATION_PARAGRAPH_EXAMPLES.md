# Consultation: Your paragraph → governance-ready points

In **Tab 1 – Your inputs**, under "Why does the Team consult VIDRU Board/ DRB/ PIB?", you can type a short paragraph (2–3 lines). **Generate with AI** converts it into structured points for **For Decision**, **For Input**, and **For Awareness**, which you can then edit.

## How it works

1. **Your paragraph (2–3 lines)** – Write what you have in mind: what needs endorsement, what you want input on, and what you’re sharing for awareness.
2. **Generate with AI** – The LLM turns that into specific, governance-ready bullet points (2 per section), with optional intro lines (e.g. "Does the GHIB endorse:").
3. **Edit** – You can change any of the generated bullets or add more lines in the textareas.

## Example paragraphs (work for any project)

**Generic (replace X, Y, Z with your specifics):**

- *We need Board endorsement for [resource/budget for study X and Y], [milestone Z] and [Medicine Profile / scenario]. We seek input on any additional considerations for [finalising current phase] and [preparation for next stage gate]. For awareness we are sharing [governance boards for this stage gate] and [early views on strategy].*

**Concrete (GHIB-style):**

- *We need the GHIB to endorse the resource and budget for the Ph2a study, DDI CYP3A4 midazolam study and VEO activities, plus C2Ph2A milestone and Medicine Profile. We’d like input on any additional considerations for the team in finalising Phase 2A plans and preparation for the next stage gate (Phase 2B combo). For awareness we’re sharing governance boards for this stage gate (ID Board, GSB and access review board) and early views on development and partnership strategy.*

**Short:**

- *Endorsement for [study name] and [milestone]. Input on risks and next stage gate. Awareness: current status and governance boards.*

**Tip:** Mention what needs **approval** (Decision), what you want **guidance** on (Input), and what you’re **informing** the board about (Awareness). The AI will map your sentences into those three sections and keep bullets specific and concise.
